Free QR Code Generator with Custom Logo

Create professional QR codes with your logo in seconds. Our free QR code generator allows you to customize your QR codes with your brand logo, perfect for business cards, marketing materials, product packaging, and event promotions. No sign up or registration required.

How to Create QR Codes for Business Cards

  1. Enter Your Information: Add your contact details, website URL, or any text you want to encode in the QR code.
  2. Upload Your Logo: Click "Select Logo" to add your company logo or personal brand to the center of the QR code.
  3. Customize Settings: Adjust the QR code size, border width, and logo size to match your design preferences.
  4. Generate & Download: Click "Generate QR Code" and download your custom QR code in PNG, SVG, or JPG format.
  5. Add to Business Cards: Import the downloaded QR code into your business card design software or send it to your printer.

Why Use QR Codes with Logos?

  • Brand Recognition: Adding your logo makes QR codes instantly recognizable and builds trust with your audience.
  • Professional Appearance: Branded QR codes look more polished and legitimate on business cards and marketing materials.
  • Higher Scan Rates: Custom QR codes with logos get scanned up to 30% more than plain black and white codes.
  • Versatile Use: Perfect for business cards, flyers, posters, product packaging, restaurant menus, and event tickets.

Multiple Download Formats

Download your custom QR code in the format that works best for your project:

  • PNG: Best for digital use on websites, social media, and email signatures. Supports transparency.
  • SVG: Vector format that scales infinitely without losing quality. Perfect for large prints, banners, and professional printing.
  • JPG: Smaller file size, ideal for email attachments and quick sharing. Works everywhere.
